Personal data is collected and processed with the explicit consent of the user, in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

We ensure that our partners handle this data securely.

Data Security

We implement security measures to protect your information: hashed passwords, HTTPS, and secure tokens.

User Rights

You have the right to access, correct, and delete your data. You can also withdraw your consent at any time via the app.
